Privacy Policy

Effective: June 25, 2026

In short:

Sanshar AI is a content channel, not a data-collecting product. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data from viewers, and we run no advertising or viewer-tracking of our own. Any platform integrations are used only to publish Sanshar AI’s own content to Sanshar AI’s own accounts.

This Privacy Policy explains how Sanshar AI handles information in connection with publishing its educational and informational content.

1. What We Do — and What We Don’t Collect

Sanshar AI publishes educational and informational content (videos, posts, and articles about AI). We do not collect, store, or sell personal data from the people who view our content, and we do not operate any advertising or behavioral-tracking systems of our own. We do not build viewer profiles.

2. Platform Integrations (Publishing Our Own Content)

To post our content, we connect to social media accounts that we own or are authorized to manage. We access those accounts through the platforms’ official APIs (for example, YouTube, TikTok, and Meta/Instagram/Facebook), using access tokens granted by the account owner. This access is used solely to create, schedule, and publish Sanshar AI’s own original content, and to read back basic aggregate metrics about our own posts (such as view and like counts) for our own analytics. It is never used to gather personal data about viewers.

3. Access Tokens & Storage

Account access tokens are stored securely and used only for the purposes above. We keep only what is necessary to operate the channel, and the connected-account owner can revoke our access at any time through the relevant platform’s settings.

4. Viewers’ Data Is Governed by the Host Platforms

When you watch or interact with our content on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook, any data you provide is collected and handled by those platforms under their own privacy policies — not by us. Please review each platform’s privacy policy to understand how it handles your information.

5. If You Contact Us

If you email us, we use your message and email address only to respond to you. We do not add you to any marketing list.

6. Children

Our content is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your Requests

If you have any question or request regarding privacy, contact us at the address below and we will respond.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest version.
