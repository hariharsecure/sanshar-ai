What Sanshar AI makes

An AI-made short film — built for $0 on local models

A narrative short produced end-to-end with locally-run open models (image, video, and voice) on a single Mac — no paid cloud generation. The episodes share both the finished result and the honest production reality: the identity-consistency fixes, the prompt-length limits, and the steps that still need a human.

Reasoning-RL research

Open notes and experiments on how to train and reward reasoning in AI systems — reward design, self-play with external verification, and the limits of self-supervision. Claims are stated with their evidence, and uncertainty is labelled rather than hidden.

A free, hands-on AI learning site

An interactive, fully static ML/AI curriculum — transformers, agents, RAG, fine-tuning, and more — that runs its computation in your browser with no backend. Built to teach the field by letting you poke at it directly.
